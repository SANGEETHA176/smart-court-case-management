Citizen (Case Admitter) Access Overview

Role Summary in Smart Court Case Management System

👩‍💼 Role Overview

As a Citizen (Case Admitter), you can easily file and manage your court cases online. This role allows users to submit new cases, track their status, upload documents, and receive real-time updates about hearings and verdicts. Below is a detailed summary of your access rights and actions available inside the app.

🏠 Dashboard

View all cases you’ve filed, track their current status, and get a quick summary of updates.

🧾 File New Case

Submit new case details with title, description, and necessary documents directly from the app.

📂 My Cases

View the complete list of your submitted cases with real-time updates and document access.

📑 Track Progress

Follow each stage of your case — from submission, verification, lawyer assignment, hearing, to final verdict.

📅 Hearing Info

Check scheduled hearing dates, court timings, and assigned locations instantly.

📎 Upload Documents

Upload additional evidence or updated files safely and securely anytime.

🔔 Notifications

Receive instant notifications about case approval, hearings, verdicts, and updates from the clerk or lawyer.

👤 Profile Management

Edit personal and contact information, update password, and manage your account securely.

🧠 Summary

The Citizen role focuses on accessibility and transparency. It gives people the power to file cases online, stay informed at every stage, and maintain direct digital communication with the court system — all without visiting the courthouse physically.
